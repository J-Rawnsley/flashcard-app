Flashcard Hint Generator

Welcome to the flashcard hint generator. This is a time-saving app for English language learners who use flashcards (either paper flashcards or flashcard software such as Anki or Brainscape) to remember words and spellings. When you learn a new word, and you want to remember that word, you can make a flashcard with the word on one side and a hint on the other. You can train your memory by looking at the hint and trying to recall the word. The bit that takes time is finding or thinking of a suitable hint for each new word. This app does that for you, to save you time and help you learn more words! The app works in two ways:

  1. Type as many words as you like into the box, separated by spaces. The app will produce a list of words and a hint for each word, which you can copy and paste into your favourite flashcard app.
  2. Type a single word into the box. In this case, the hint generator will give you a selection of different hints for that word, so you can choose your favourite (note that some words have only one hint available).

The hints are generated using Words API
